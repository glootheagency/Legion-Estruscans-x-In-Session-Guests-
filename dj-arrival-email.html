Overview

Thanks for being part of the night. Below is everything you need to arrive, load in, and perform. Please read in full before Thursday.

Steven will be on-site setting up from 3 pm. You're welcome to arrive anytime between 5 and 6 pm. Music starts at 6 pm sharp in all three rooms.

Arrival & Load In

Getting There

Enter Lincoln Park at 34th Avenue and Clement. Continue straight past the front of the museum and make a LEFT onto El Camino de Mar. The Loading Dock Gate is on the left — ring the buzzer for access.

All DJs must check in with security at the Loading Dock before proceeding. Security is inside the hallway at the loading dock — buzz the door if not open. Do not enter via the Terrace Glass Doors or Court of Honor Gate without checking in first.

Once unloaded, re-park on El Camino de Mar (free, unmetered). No vehicles may remain at the dock or terrace driveway.

  • Arrive between 5 – 6 pm (Steven on-site from 3 pm)
  • Check in at Loading Dock security before anything else
  • Sound check 5 – 5:45 pm (ideal but not mandatory)
  • Music starts at 6 pm in all three rooms
  • Re-park after unloading — no vehicles at dock
